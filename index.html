About us 👥

Singapore's first premium yet affordable gym 🏋

Started as a small gym located at the top of a multi-storey carpark in October 2010. GYMMBOXX has progressed and expanded into 7 gyms island-wide. Their target group is the mass heartlanders thus they are mostly located near residential neighbourhoods. Gymmboxx is still striving to expand and bring high quality gym facilities and services to cater to a wider target audience.
