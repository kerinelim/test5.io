How-to ❔





Perform a Squat correctly

Step 1: Stand straight with feet shoulder-width apart
Step 2: Tighten your core
Step 3: Lower down as if sitting on a chair until your thighs are parallel to the ground
Step 4: Straighten your legs to lift back up

Muscles targeted: Quadriceps, Hamstrings, Gluteals



















Perform a Anterior Deltoid Raise correctly

Step 1: Stand straight with feet shoulder-width apart while holding a dumbbell with both hands
Step 2: Keep your arms straight with your elbows slightly bent
Step 3: Lift the weights to shoulder height
Step 4: Lower back down to the starting position

Muscles targeted: Anterior Deltoid






Perform a Upright Row correctly

Step 1: Stand straight with feet shoulder-width apart
Step 2: Grip the barbell slightly closer than shoulder-width with arms extended
Step 3: Lift barbell up leader with elbows until arms are parallel to the floor (keep the weight close to your body)
Step 4: Lower back down to the starting position

Muscles targeted: Trapezius, Deltoids, Biceps
